History

Founded in 2007 by renowned musician and educator, Professor Jarod Barton. From its humble beginnings in a room, the school has grown exponentially, now offering a wide range of programs and classes to aspiring musicians of all ages and skill levels. With a focus on Filipino classical and contemporary music, Master's Creator School has cultivated a reputation for producing highly skilled and versatile musicians who have gone on to achieve success in their careers.

Philosophy

We nurture individuality, celebrate creativity, and embrace diverse musical traditions. Through holistic development, collaboration, and a lifelong love for music, we empower students to become master creators in their own right.

Mission

To develop child as an independent learner. To enhance his/her knowledge through modern technique of teaching children. To increase his/her intellectual and language competence and to provide him/her with the skills of self-expression.

Vision

Equipping young leaders of tomorrow through quality education that respond to the needs of the society as country loving and responsible citizens of the nation.

Goals

  1. Develop a comprehensive curriculum that covers various music styles, theory, technique, and performance skills.
  2. Foster a supportive and inspiring learning environment that encourages students to excel in their musical pursuits.
  3. Offer specialized programs or classes for advanced students who show exceptional talent and dedication.
  4. Encourage students to explore their creative potential through composition, improvisation, and other musical expressions.
  5. Provide performance opportunities such as recitals, concerts, and competitions to showcase students' abilities and build confidence.
  6. Organize community outreach programs to expose a wider audience to music, such as workshops, demonstrations, or free concerts.
  7. Collaborate with local schools or organizations to promote music education and highlight the benefits of music in academic and personal development.
